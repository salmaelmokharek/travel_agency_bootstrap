Coastal Adventure Tour

Discover Morocco's stunning coastline, from historic ports to beautiful beaches and charming seaside towns.

  • 6 Days / 5 Nights
  • Surf & Beach Activities
  • Seafood Cuisine
Day 1: Arrival in Essaouira, exploring the historic medina and port.
Day 2: Surfing lesson, beach activities, and local seafood lunch.
Day 3: Day trip to Oualidia, known for oysters and tranquil lagoons.
Day 4: Coastal hiking, visit to traditional fishing villages.
Day 5: Water sports, cooking class featuring local seafood cuisine, farewell dinner.
Sahara Desert Expedition

Explore the magical Sahara with camel treks, overnight camps, and breathtaking desert landscapes.

  • 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Desert Camping
  • Stargazing Experience
Day 1: Departure from Marrakech, driving through the High Atlas Mountains.
Day 2: Arrival at Merzouga, camel trek into the Sahara Desert, sunset views.
Day 3: Full day of desert exploration, traditional Berber camp experience.
Day 4: Morning desert sunrise, sandboarding, cultural encounters with local nomads.
Day 5: Return journey, stopping at historic kasbahs and scenic viewpoints.
